Everything you need to know about the BullishFX ecosystem.

You can start learning with any amount. For live trading, we recommend a minimum of $200-$500 to practice proper risk management, or using a prop firm challenge.

Our curriculum is designed to take a complete beginner to a professional level using Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and institutional price action.

We focus on **education**, not signals. We teach you how to fish so you can be independent for the rest of your life, though we do share weekly market breakdowns.

We primarily use TradingView for analysis and MetaTrader 4 or 5 for executing trades.
